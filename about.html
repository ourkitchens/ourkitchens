Our Story

It started in a home kitchen, not a restaurant.

Chef Sleiman began cooking the way most great food starts — for family, on weekends, with whatever was fresh at the market that morning. What began as trays passed between neighbors slowly grew into requests for gatherings, then weddings, then full events.

Today, Our Kitchen is a small team that still cooks the same way: fresh, unhurried, and generous. We haven't changed the recipes — we've just made room for more people at the table.

Our Mission

Food that feels like it was made for you, specifically.

We believe catering shouldn't feel mass-produced. Our mission is to bring the warmth of a home-cooked meal to every event we serve — whether that's a boardroom lunch for ten or a wedding for three hundred.

That means fresh ingredients, honest portions, and menus built around what your guests will actually enjoy — not just what's easy to scale.
